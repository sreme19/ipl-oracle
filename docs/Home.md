## How it works in three sentences

`ipl-oracle run --team RCB` resolves the next fixture, loads squad and venue data, and passes both through a 10-step pipeline of specialised agents. A MILP solver selects the best 11 players subject to IPL role and overseas constraints; a Monte Carlo simulator (10,000 ball-by-ball rollouts) and an MDP chase model estimate win probability. The result is a recommended XI with per-player slot reasons, a toss call, and an optional plain-English strategy brief generated by Claude.
