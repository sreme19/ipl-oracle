# LSG Analyst Briefing (2026)

## Analyst Take
LSG are typically viewed as strategically flexible with broad bowling options and a high-upside top six, but recurrent questions remain around opening stability.

## Strengths
- Bowling variety across pace and spin
- Adaptable composition by venue
- Capable finishers for close chases

## Risks
- Opening partnerships can be unstable
- Can become over-reliant on overseas batting output
